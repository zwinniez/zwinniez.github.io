OUT_4_PIZZA

Kai Jin and Winnie Zheng

Laura Brothers creates digital experiences through her LiveJournal blog, out_4_pizza.com. Her work is heavily inspired by the early ages of the internet, emphasizing the pixelated display.

Brothers is one of the first net artists to utilize scrolling and zooming, which takes effective advantage of tools exclusive to the online user. These interactive elements create the stimulating motion of the moire effect.
