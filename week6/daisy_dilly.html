Brothers explores "painting" with the computer in her art. Her work embraces the organic roots of digital pixel display. Her aesthetic integrates both that and the moire effect, creating a unique effect.

Her most recent work explores the illusion of moving digitalization of 2-dimensional media art. She allows her viewers to interact with her static art by combining the illusion of the moire effect with human computer interaction of the digital world.
